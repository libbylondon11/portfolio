Think Dream Tank

Think Dream Tank

I worked for the Think Dream Tank as a freelance designer and developer. I worked with the client and a small team of developers. Technologies used: Illustrator, Bootstrap, Angular.js, Node.js.

Bulk Powder

Bulk Powder App

I designed and developed this site as a resource for people that have unlabeled bulk items at home and wish to identify what they are. The website helps users identify and label these items based on PLU number and grocery store. I developed this site as a solo project when I was a student at Prime Digital Academy.

Nile of the North

North Nile

We designed this to help Project Sweetie Pie, a local non-profit based in north Minneapolis that supports young people in the neighborhood in gaining valuable farming skills. We created an easy to use map that features a responsive map of food resources. Community members are encouraged to add their own resources to the map.

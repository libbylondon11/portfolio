Center for Patient and Provider Experience

MY ROLE

UX/UI Design, Wireframes, Mobile Design, Branding

THE CHALLENGE

The Center for Patient and Provider Experience (CPPE) is a group of medical doctors that conduct research around provider burn-out. They needed a website to share their research with medical doctors and researchers internationally and nationally in the field. They wanted to collect users' data before they could download research materials and they also wanted branding and a cohesive and inviting experience for their users when visiting their site.

THE SOLUTION

We created a mobile-responsive website for a group of medical researchers that wanted their research to be accessed easily and utilized by other medical doctors and reseachers.

THE PROCESS

We started out by going through a discovery mapping process with the staff to better understand what the purpose of their new website meant to everyone. Through this process we were able to understand that accessibility, ease of use, and simplicity were critical for busy doctors looking to access research quickly while on the go. This helped us create user personas. After that, we created a simple wireframe for the application focusing on mobile-first design, easy-to-find-research, and a system for collecting user data. We created multiple prototypes rapidly after that so we could iterate with the client. The client and I were pleased that the site made their research much more accessible and easily shared and used.

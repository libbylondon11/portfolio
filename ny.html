Second Harvest Heartland Annual Report 2016

THE CHALLENGE

To design an impactful annual report that incorporated real client stories from 2016, innovations and programatic changes within Second Harvest Heartland, and the impact that the organizaion had on ending hunger in Minnesota and Western Wisconsin.

THE SOLUTION

To create the highest level of impact, a combination of graphic design as well as client images and quotes was utilized using responsive digital design. Interactive media aimed at including the user in exploring and learning more about hunger was also a high priority for the design, and polls, quizzes, true/false questions, infographics, and facts were incorporated whenever possible. A corresponding printed version was included in the Pioneer Press as well.

IMPACT

Through analytical tracking, we found that this report had 40% more click rates and engagement than the previous year. This was most likely due to social media promotions of the report, more impactful stories, and email promotions of the report throughout the year.

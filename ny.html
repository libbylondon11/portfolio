Nearest You

MY ROLE

Wireframes, Heuristic Reviews, UX Research, UX/UI Design, Mobile Design, Usability Testing

THE CHALLENGE

Nearest You is an online application that connects users with local products closest to them. Their Beta site was not mobile-friendly, accessibly to varrying user groups, their design was inconsistent, and their content was disorganized. They wanted local vendors to be able to easily join the site and sign up, and they also wanted to have product pages clearly highlight the cool local products while also maintaining Nearest You branding guidelines. They came to us wanting a total usability overhaul based in UX principles.

THE SOLUTION

We designed a mobile-responsive application that was designed first with the user in mind and second with the vendors in mind. Since searching for local products is the main feature of the site, we made sure that that was more easily recognizable feature to all user groups. We designed an intuitive "join us" drop down button and page for vendors to learn more about becoming a member. We redesigned the product pages to incorporate a visual map of the product locations and used the same branding as the other Nearest You main pages.

THE PROCESS

I started out by conducting a heuristic review of the website to better understand how it currently functioned. I then researched similar sites and applications by creating a competitive review to better understand the market and users. I then researched comparative sites with similar missions or functionality but not necessarily meeting the same needs as the competitive market. We then conducted usability research via sending surveys to users as well as business owners with local products. This informed our user persona design and we moved into UI. We designed the site first by sketching wireframes on paper and then transfering them to Axure and incorporating functionality.

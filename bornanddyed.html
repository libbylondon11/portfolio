BORN & DYED IN MN




Challenge

Born and Dyed in MN is a local dyed farm I ran. The business was based in a young farmer cooperative near Minneapolis. The business ran classes and currently sells natural dye plants and seeds for artists and recreational dyers. The business needed branding and a logo for merchandise and marketing.

My Role

My Role: UX Wireframing, Usability Testing, User Research, Mobile Design

Process

I has to understand the users of the products; this involved research among field of similar sustainable farm businesses with niche products as well as researching arts and crafts businesses. Once the "look and feel" was established based on this research, I was able to brand and design around that.

Time Constraint

1 month

Think Dream Tank

Think Dream Tank is a crowd sourced and community led project that aims to create a space where people from any background can dream about their future and share a conversation about their hopes with others.
















Nile of the North

We designed this to help Project Sweetie Pie, a local non-profit based in north Minneapolis that supports young people in the neighborhood in gaining valuable farming skills. We created an easy to use map that features a responsive map of food resources. Community members are encouraged to add their own resources to the map.

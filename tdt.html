Think Dream Tank

MY ROLE

Wireframes, Heuristic Reviews, UX Research, UX/UI Design, Mobile Design, Usability Testing

THE CHALLENGE

Think Dream Tank is a crowd sourced and community led project that aims to create a space where people from any background can dream about their future and share a conversation about their hopes with others. The Think Dream Tank team wanted a website that would allow people from all around the world to share their hopes and dreams for the future. They wanted to track and measure these dreams and goals as part of their research; and knowing exactly where a person is when they submit these dreams and hopes was important to them. They also wanted to make sure that users could interact and engage with other people's hopes and dreams.

THE SOLUTION

We designed and created a mapping website that would allow people from all around the world to share their hopes and dreams for the future. The client wanted to track and measure these dreams and goals as part of their research They wanted to know exactly where a person is when they submit these dreams and hopes. They also wanted to make sure that users could interact and engage with other people's hopes and dreams. We saw this as an opportunity to design and code a highly functional site that could manage users log-in information, track goals and dreams, and visually show the breadth of these dreams via an interactive map.

THE PROCESS

We started out by interviewing the researchers at Think Dream Tank about how they wanted to collect the data and who would be using the site. From there we created wireframes of the site and the database. Once the wireframes were completed, we created the site using angular.js, node.js and javascript.

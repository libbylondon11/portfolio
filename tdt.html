THINK DREAM TEAM




Project

Think Dream Tank is a crowd sourced and community led project that aims to create a space where people from any background can dream about their future and share a conversation about their hopes with others.

Challenge

The Think Dream Tank team wanted a website that would allow people from all around the world to share their hopes and dreams for the future. They wanted to track and measure these dreams and goals as part of their research; and knowing exactly where a person is when they submit these dreams and hopes was important to them. They also wanted to make sure that users could interact and engage with other people's hopes and dreams.

Opportunity

We saw this as an opportunity to design and code a highly functional site that could manage users log in information, track goals and dreams, and would use a mapping api.

My Role

UX Design, Front and Backend Development, Database Management

Development

We used the MEAN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, Angular.js, and Node.js) to create the website.

Permaculture Research Institute - Cold Climate (PRI)

MY ROLE

UX Wireframing, Usability Testing, User Research, Mobile Design

The Challenge

The Permaculture Research Institute - Cold Climate is a non-profit based in Minneapolis, MN, that supports sustainable agriculture and local farms in the midwest. They had a website that was old, challenging to use, and had lots of content that needed to be refined and curated.

The Solution

We overhauled the current website and created a new, easy to use, and mobile responsive site.

The Process

I interviewed PRI members, staff, and board of directors to create user personas to guide our design process. Based on the user personas, I created a content strategy, and prioritized accessibility because I found that many users of the site were over the age of 60 or were using the site on their mobile devices. I created wireframes based on these interviews and started designing the new site.

Analysis and Process

After desiging and creating the beta site, I created and conducted user surveys and usability tests. I targeted our various user groups to begin to understand how the site worked for them. The usability test was completed by 10 people and all feedback was analyzed and incorporated into the final site.
